About SHAUN


As a Physique/Strength Based Coach I am looking to change the lives of everyone and anyone interested in living a healthier lifestyle. I work with a Range of clients from beginners to the fitness industry all the way up to the more advanced athlete wanting to compete in Bodybuilding or Powerlifting Events.

I have been within the fitness industry for 6 years now and have progressed as a Personal Trainer/Coach with each year continuing to further my knowledge within the industry so I can offer the highest standard of coaching possible to all of my clients/athletes and have them reaching their specific goals as fast and as efficient as possible.

I am a Pro Natural Bodybuilder competing in my first Season in 2017 and winning my Pro card through the ICN in my third show, I have a background in amateur powerlifting and continually looking to further progress in both Physique and Strength based events in the future as I continue to grow within the industry and help as many people as I can along the way reach their own respective goals.
